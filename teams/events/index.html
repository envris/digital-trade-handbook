Guilds, showcases and events

The people in delivery teams regularly meet other people working in the same role. These events are called 'guilds' or 'communities of practice'.

Guilds are used to work on shared problems. They are also used to improve the ways of working for different disciplines.

There are other regular events focused on types of work and stages.

Some of these events are limited to TFTM, others are open to the whole department.
